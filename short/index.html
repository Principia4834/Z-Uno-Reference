A short is a 16-bit data-type. This yields a range of -32,768 to 32,767 (minimum value of -2^15 and a maximum value of (2^15) - 1). 
    
   short ledPin = 13;
    
  
    
         short var = val;

var - your short variable name

val - the value you assign to that variable
